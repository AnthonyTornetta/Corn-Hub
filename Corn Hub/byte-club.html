Byte Club!

About

The Byte Club, founded in 2014 by the AP computer science class, is a Computer Science club dedicated to the advancement of student knowledge in all fields of Computer Science. Club members work together to complete individual projects over the course of the year. These projects are completely up to the group members and focus on anything from game development to app creation. The sky is the limit with Computer Science.

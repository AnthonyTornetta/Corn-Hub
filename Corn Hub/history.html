Ulysses S. Grant

4/12/61

~Confederates attack Fort Sumter~

I knew this would happen, I knew that the Confederates would strike at us.

This war was never avoidable because of the idiocy of slavery

it needs to be abolished!

I just can't beleive that they would attack first.

I mean, we are superior in almost every way over the confederates!

You know why they got the first strike? Because we are cowards!

I need to volenteer for the army! Make my country proud!

I'm going to volenteer for the military to fight off the traitorous Confederates!

I, Ulysses Simpson Grant, will make the Union proud!

10/22/62

~Emancipation Proclamation~

It is about time that slavery was outlawed for the Confederates!

This has not only weakened the south, but has freed the slaves!

Today is truely a great day in history!

Although the Union has been losing battles, this might just turn the

tide in our favor!

4/4/63

~Vicksburg~

This was by far one of my most important victories for the Union

By capturing Vicksburg, I managed to cut off their main supply route for the rest of the Confederates

After capturing Port Gibson, I was able to cut off Fort Vicksburg's supplies by destroying rail roads and surround

the port until they finally surrendered.

Throughout this battle I managed to outsmart the leading commander of the Confederate, Pemberton, by

doing the exact opposite of what he expected.

After ages of combat, they finally agreed to unconditional surrender.

7/4/63

~Pickett's Charge~

I heard that, thankfully, General Meade was able to drive off Lee's forces from invading the Gettysburg.

To my knowledge, this is the first loss on Lee's side since this war began!

Maybe this could mean a turn of tide in this never ending war.

Although it was still frightening how far they got on Union soil...

7/18/63

~New York Draft Riots~

The people in the North who oppose the draft are fools!

Who wouldn't want to support the Union in this war?

The draft is nessesary absolutely nessesary to ensure our victory in this civil war.

If I were drafted, I would serve my country loyally!

12/21/64

~Sherman's March~

I think it's about time that we had a General (besides me)

who knows what they'r doing!

His tactics are great because they scare the Confederates and

feed his troops for free!

I'm also extremely happy that McClellan is not longer in command

He was just terrible...

4/9/65

~Lee Surrenders~

Although I should be celebrating today, upon Lee's surrender, I feel saddness.

The deep saddness that only those who faught their brothers would understand

Nevertheless, I am glad that Lee surrendered to me and that this dreadful war is finally over

even though they were given conditions upon their surrender.

It took me about 4 years of wearing down their army until they finally surrendered.

I hope that the Union can recover from this war, as that we don't have another battle of

this magnitude again.

4/15/65

~Lincoln's Assasination~

I can't believe it... the war was finally over!

And for this... scoundrel to assassinate Lincoln?

It's insane! The war was finally over! The peace process was just starting!

What kind of a traitor could have done this.

This will put the peace process far behind in the peace making process with this

idiot of a Vice President Andrew Johnson!

This could lead to disunion within the Union...

Corn Hub - Fix Firefox

Note: Do the following steps in the FireFox browser!

9/10 times this is the problem:

If your firefox is not loading sites such as google or youtube, this is most likely a certificate error (thanks Cedar Crest).

To correct this error, Corn Hub has provided a nice lil' link for you to install the certificate.

Simply click 'install certificate' while you're in firefox and check the first box then hit ok.

Google, youtube, and your other sites will now work.
